DefaultDateRangeJob

trait DefaultDateRangeJob extends Job

Sets up an implicit dateRange to use in your sources and an implicit timezone. Example args: --date 2011-10-02 2011-10-04 --tz UTC If no timezone is given, Pacific is assumed.

    Rather than give the full power of cascading's selectors, we have a simpler set of rules encoded below: 1) if the input is non-definite (ALL, GROUP, ARGS, etc...) ALL is the output.
